On the tranfer page, you can transfer funds from one account to another.
  1. Select the account from which you wish to transfer from the list labeled From Account.
  2. Select the account to which you wish to transfer from the list labeled To Account.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to transfer into the Amount field. This must be a positive numeric value.
  4. If you wish, you can enter a description in the Memo field. This description will show up in your account's history.
  5. Once you are ready to make the transfer, click the OK button or press Enter on the keyboard.
