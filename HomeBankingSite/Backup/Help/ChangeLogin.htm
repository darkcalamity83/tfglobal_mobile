To change your Username or Password,
  1. Enter your current Username into the Current Username field.
  2. Enter your current Passsword into the Current Password field.
  3. Enter the new Username into the New Username field. If you do not want a new Username, enter your old Username into the New Username field.
  4. Enter the new Password into the New Password field. If you do not want a new Password, enter your old Password into the New Password field.
  5. Enter the new Password into the Confirm New Password field. If you do not want a new Password, enter your old Password into the Confirm New Password field.
  6. Click OK or press the Enter key on your keyboard.
