On the stop checks page, you can request stop payment on a group of check numbers or on a check amount. The credit union has the option to deny your requet. To do this, follow the following instructions:
  1. Select the account from which you wish to stop payment on from the combo box.
  2. You can enter a start check number in the Start Check field and a end check number in the End Check field or enter the amount you wish to stop payment on in the Amount field.
  3. Once you are ready to make the stop payment request, click the OK button or press Enter on the keyboard.
  4. Remember this is only a request and the credit union can deny it.
