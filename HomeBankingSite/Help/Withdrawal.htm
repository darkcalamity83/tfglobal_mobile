On the withdrawal page, you can withdrawl funds from one of your accounts. This will print a check at the Credit Union, which will then be mailed to you. To do this, follow the following instructions:
  1. Select the account from which you wish to withdrawal from the list labeled From Account.
  2. In the Amount field, enter the amount you wish to withdrawal. This must be a positive numeric value.
  3. If you wish, you can enter a description in the Memo field. This description will show up in your account's history.
  4. Once you are ready to make the withdrawal, click the OK button or press Enter on the keyboard.
