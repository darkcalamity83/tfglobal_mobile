To activate your account, do the following.
  1. Enter your account number in the Account # field.
  2. Enter your activation code in the activation code field. Your activation code should have been assigned to you by your credit union. If you do not remember your activation code, please visit your nearest branch.
  3. Enter your desired username in the username field. This must be a unique value of at least 6 characters long. If your username already exists, try modifying it by appending numbers to the end.
  4. Enter your password in the password and confirm password field. Password must be at least 6 characters long and must contain at least one letter and one number.
  5. Enter a valid Email into the Email Field
  6. Enter a Keyword that only you know so when you log in you know your in the right place.
  7. Fill out the three sequrity questions, write them down and keep them in a safe place.
  8. When done click the image you wish to use to activate your account.
  9. Note: If you are logging on between 11:45pm and 1:30am, the Home Banking site is unavailable due to nightly maintentance.
